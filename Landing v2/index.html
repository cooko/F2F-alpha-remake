Farm-To-Fork

Login/ Signup
Farm-to-fork is dedicated to helping you improve food security in your community.


Farm-to-Fork at its core is a means to connect you guys, the community, with those in need. It is a centralized location for local community food providers to post and maintain a running list of their weekly needs as well as provide a means for you to easily pledge to fulfill them. By increasing the visibility of the needs in our community we hope to increase not only the quantity of donations but also the quality. So join us in helping to eliminate food insecurity in our community!
